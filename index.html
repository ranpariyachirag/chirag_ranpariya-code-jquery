03-25-2021

Red Level Circuit Breaker Announced for Section of Zone 4 (E

Effective at 6 p.m. ADT, a Red Level circuit breaker has been announced for a section of Zone 4 (Edmundston region), including Edmundston and the upper Madawaska region, for at least 4 days. Dr. Jennifer Russell, chief medical officer of health, has said this measure could be extended for a longer period of time and expanded to cover the rest of Zone 4, depending on test findings.

Mass testing for asymptomatic people is scheduled today and tomorrow to help determine how widespread the outbreak is.

All other zones, including the communities outside the circuit breaker in Zone 4, remain in the Yellow Level under the province’s mandatory order.
02-19-2021

New Brunswick Zone 4 Moving to Orange Level Effective February 19

Effective February 19, Zone 4 (Edmundston region) has joined the rest of New Brunswick in moving back to the Orange Level in the province’s COVID-19 recovery plan
01-25-2021

NB Requirements: Active Workplace Screening in Red and Lockdown Regions

An updated mandatory order was released over the weekend. It implements many changes with regards to COVID-19 protocols across New Brunswick. The most significant changes relate to the current lockdown in Zone 4 (Edmundston area) and Level Red in Zones 1-3 (Moncton, Saint John, and Fredericton areas).
